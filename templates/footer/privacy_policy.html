Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st of January 2025

Last Updated: 10th of February 2025

Welcome to Tracklio! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you access or use our platform. By using Tracklio, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register on Tracklio, we collect details like your username, hashed password and login credentials to manage your account securely.
  • Expense Data: Information you add related to your expenses, such as date, amount, and descriptions.
  • Usage Data: Data on how you interact with Tracklio, including device type, browser information, IP address, and usage patterns to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your Tracklio account.
  • To securely store and manage your expense data.
  • To generate personalized reports and visualizations for better financial insights.
  • To improve the performance, features, and security of our platform.

3. Data Security

We take strong technical and organizational measures to protect your data. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work to safeguard your personal information through encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With trusted service providers who help us operate and maintain Tracklio (under strict confidentiality agreements).
  • When required by law, legal proceedings, or government requests.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of Tracklio, its users, or the public.

5. Your Rights

As a Tracklio user, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to applicable laws.
  • Control how your data is processed, including opting out of non-essential communications.

6. Cookies

Tracklio uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences, improve functionality, and analyze usage. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may be limited as a result.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications. Continued use of Tracklio after updates means you accept the revised policy.
